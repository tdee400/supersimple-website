Learn how many calories you burn daily with our easy, no-nonsense TDEE calculator.

TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure) represents the total number of calories your body burns in a day, accounting for your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) and activity level. Our calculator uses the Mifflin-St Jeor Equation, a scientifically validated method for estimating caloric needs based on gender, age, weight, height, and activity level.
